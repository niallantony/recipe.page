Simple Hummus

Ingredients

  • Dry Chickpeas 400g
  • Flatleaf Parsley
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • Olive Oil
  • Paprika

Method

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight in water with a little bit of Baking Soda
  2. Next day, boil the chickpeas for around 2 hours, add a star anise or two for a little extra flavour
  3. In a food processor, pulse the toasted sesame seeds to a fine paste
  4. Add the cooked chickpeas to the food processor, do not throw away the water
  5. Pulse the chickpeas, adding a little bit of water at a time until you get a consistency you are happy with (I like mine a little chunky)
  6. Spread on a flat plate and make a well in the middle, pour the olive oil into the well, sprinkle a little paprika over and garnish with parsley. Eat with toasted flat bread.
