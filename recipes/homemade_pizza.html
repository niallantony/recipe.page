Simple Hummus

Ingredients

  • Strong Flour 3 cups
  • Yeast
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Pasata
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Basil

Method

  1. Make the dough a couple of hours in advance. Mix yeast with one and a half cups of water and let it soak for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the flour to a mixing bowl and combine with one tablespoon of fine salt.
  3. Add the water to the flour slowly, mixing to combine into a dough. Add two tablespoons of olive oil too
  4. Knead until a smooth and bouncy dough is formed. Oil another mixing bowl, add the dough ball and let rise for 1 hour
  5. After the dough has doubled in size, knead a little more and form into two balls. Cover and let rise for another 30 minutes
  6. Preheat a heavy baking tray or pizza stone in the oven.
  7. Take each ball of dough and work it into a thin disk. Begin using a rolling pin and then tease it wider with your hands
  8. Spread a couple of spoons of tomato pasata on top, sprinkle with Mozarella cheese and add leaves of basil
  9. Put either directly onto the tray/pizza tone, alternatively sprinkle a little semolina flour and put on top of that
  10. Cook for ten minutes and eat hot
