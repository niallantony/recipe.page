Pesto Pasta

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Basil Pesto
  • Cherry Tomatoes 200g
  • Half a large onion
  • Any kind of mushrooms 200g

Method

  1. Bring a pan of heavily salted water to the boil and put the spaghetti in
  2. Chop the onions, mushrooms and tomatoes to roughly the same size
  3. Heat oil in a large pan and begin frying the onions and mushrooms
  4. After five minutes throw in the tomatoes
  5. Add a large dollop of pasta and stir to combine
  6. By now the spaghetti should be cooked al dente so lift it into the frying pan, as well as a large spoonful of pasta water
  7. Cook for a minute more and serve immediately.
